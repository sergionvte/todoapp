Manage your tasks with TodoApp

Discover simplicity in task management with TodoApp. Designed to make your life easier, this minimalist application, built with Django, Tailwind CSS, and backed by MySQL, provides you with a quiet space to organize your tasks efficiently.

Key Features:

  • Simple Task List: Create and manage your tasks quickly and straightforwardly.
  • Swift Registration: Join with just a few clicks and start organizing your days.
  • Reliable MySQL: Your information is secure and accessible thanks to our MySQL database.
  • Clean Design with Tailwind CSS: Enjoy a clean and functional interface with the simple styles of Tailwind CSS.

Getting Started:

  1. Sign Up: Create an account in seconds.
  2. Add Tasks: Simplify your life by organizing your tasks effortlessly.
  3. Manage Your Day: Mark completed tasks and keep a clear record.
